# anambraunionmk
This is the website of Anambra Union MK CIC. It belongs to the indigenes of Anambra State of Nigeria. Uniting the sons and daughters of Anambra State — the cradle of Igbo civilisation — in Milton Keynes in particular, and United Kingdom in general. We preserve our heritage, empower our community, and build a proud bridge between home and diaspora.
